# Squishy Fish

**A game by Freya Nesbitt-Smith (age 7 1/4)**

Dive into the ocean as a scuba diver and squish as many fish as you can! But watch out -- some fish bite back, and the sea is full of dangers.

## How to play

- **Arrow keys** (up, down, left, right) to swim
- **Space bar** to squish fish you're touching (hold it down for big fish)
- **< or ,** to throw a fireball
- **> or .** to throw a freezeball

## Rules

- Squish fish with **closed mouths** for points -- bigger fish need more squishes
- **Avoid** fish with open mouths, sharks, sea urchins, pointy rocks and crabs
- **Blue seaweed** is poisonous -- don't touch!
- **Green seaweed** gives you an extra life (max 10)
- Find the **treasure chest** (guarded by rocks and urchins) for 250 bonus points, full health, 3 fireballs and 3 freezeballs
- **Sharks** take 15 space-bar hits to kill (500 points!) -- or blast them with 3 fireballs
- **Freezeballs** freeze enemies for 5 seconds so you can squish them safely
- There are **6 levels**, each a different colour, with more enemies and obstacles

## Running

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step, no dependencies.
